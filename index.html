Spring is the season following winter, marking a time of renewal and growth with warmer temperatures, budding trees, and blooming plants. It is typically considered to begin with the vernal equinox, which occurred on March 20, 2025, at 5:01 a.m. ET. In Kathmandu, located in the Northern Hemisphere, spring is transitioning from winter to summer, with temperatures gradually rising.
